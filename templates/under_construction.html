Personal Space Weather Station Network

The PSWS Network at pswsnetwork.eng.ua.edu is currently undergoing build-out.

We are currently building out the PSWS Network platform to support automated station analysis and magnetometer data visualization.

The Station analysis platform will bring together information about stations, including observations, station metadata, and instrument details, to support research and exploration of space weather conditions.

The magnetometer-graphing system will allow researchers and station operators to compare measurements across different instruments and geographic regions, providing insight into localized disturbances and broader space weather activity.

This page is a temporary placeholder while we complete development of the station-analysis tools and the magnetometer data plotting.
